I have a fair amount of experience in digital writing, mainly by using it in my daily life. The Vertical Component of the T-shaped student:

The vertical component of the "T" has to do with disciplinary depth. It means that a student has a strong understanding and strength in a certain discipline. Since we pursue specific majors in college, the "T" usually refers to how we understand the knowledge we gain through our major. I certainly have worked hard in the classes related to my majors - BIT and Creative Writing. I have a solid understanding of finding IT solutions for business problems and how supply chains and operations function. I have excelled in my classes, gone to office hours to gain additional knowledge, and done research on my own time to learn coding languages and more about IT in general. I also have strong knowledge of creative writing. I have taken classes ranging from fiction to poetry to critical reading to playwriting, and I have tried my hardest in these classes. I attend lectures given by speakers whenever I am available, to gain even more knowledge in these fields. I have compiled many portfolios of my poetry, short stories, essays, and play scripts, and I continue to refer to the feedback I received on these portfolios when I write in my classes today.

The Horizontal Component of the T-shaped student:

The T-component's horizontal aspect focuses on our interdisciplinary skills. I have complemented these skills by expanding my knowledge as well - I have done so by taking classes in project management, humanities, ecology and other sciences, technical writing, reading up on information technology during my own time, and more as a way to expand my knowledge even further and be able to look at certain topics through a different lens.

The V of the VT-shaped Student: The V-component of the student-shaped model is my favorite component, because it allows me to implement the passion and the drive that I have. In the V-component, we have the opportunity to apply the knowledge we have learned to the real world and provides us with a different form of learning. I enjoy this component because it allows us to pursue a passion
